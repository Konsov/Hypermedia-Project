Who we are

Our association was born from a group of young people who cared about the well-being and health of their city. Thanks to their commitment and their desire to get involved, they have managed to join a large number of volunteers of all ages with the aim of safeguarding cities from degradation.

Eco Vibes SA

Eco Vibes SA, società fondata nel 1988 con sedi a Locarno e Lugano, è attiva nei campi delle consulenze ambientali e naturalistiche, dell’ingegneria forestale come pure della fisica ed energetica della costruzione.
